## Features wanted, drawn from each app

**From Splitwise:**
- Item-level expense splitting — assign specific items on a bill to specific people, not just even splits
- Analytics/charts showing spending patterns over time
- Detailed, searchable expense history

**From Tricount:**
- Zero-friction start — no mandatory account creation to begin using it
- One person can create a group and share a link with others
- Clean, simple interface
- Good multi-currency support (useful for travel)

**From Spliit:**
- Open-source approach / transparent about how it works
- Privacy-first — no data selling, no ads
- Free with no artificial paywalls or feature-gating
